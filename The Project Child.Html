Who is Max Verstappen

Max Emilian Verstappen Dutch pronunciation: is a Belgian and Dutch[3] racing driver competing in Formula One, where he is the 2021, 2022, and 2023 World Champion. He races under the Dutch flag in Formula One for Red Bull Racing. Verstappen is the son of former Formula One driver Jos Verstappen, and former go-kart racer Sophie Kumpen. He had a successful run in karting and single-seater categories including FIA European Formula 3breaking several record.

2023 Season: A season for the record books

Verstappen remained with Red Bull alongside Sergio Pérez for the 2023 season. Verstappen achieved consistently high results throughout the season, setting several Formula One records, including his win at the Hungarian Grand Prix, which broke the record for most consecutive wins by a constructor at twelve, previously held by McLaren in 1988 with eleven,[240][241] and the Italian Grand Prix, which marked his tenth consecutive win, breaking Sebastian Vettel's previous record of nine set in 2013. His ten consecutive wins include a grand slam at the 2023 Spanish Grand Prix. After a brief slump in Singapore - which saw him finish fifth and his record-setting streak of ten consecutive wins be broken - Verstappen took pole position once again in Suzuka, the track where he achieved his 2022 title, and led the field ahead of Lando Norris and Oscar Piastri to secure Red Bull Racing its sixth Constructors' Championship title - and also Red Bull's second consecutive title after its victory in 2022.During the sprint for the following Qatar Grand Prix, Verstappen finished second from Oscar Piastri while Pérez did not finish, handing the 2023 Drivers' Championship to Verstappen,who joined Juan Manuel Fangio, Jack Brabham, Graham Hill, Keke Rosberg, and Nelson Piquet as the only drivers to have won a championship on a Saturday.
